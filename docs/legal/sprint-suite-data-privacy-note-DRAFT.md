This is a plain-English summary of what data Sprint Suite handles.

**What we process**
- **Sign-in (hub):** your email address and sign-in sessions, used only to authenticate you and to send magic-link sign-in emails. Email is sent via [Resend].
- **Sprintsignal:** survey responses are collected **anonymously** — answers are not linked to an identifiable person, and only team-level summaries are shown. Team and survey names you enter are stored.
- **Sprintpoker / Sprintretro:** room/board content, estimates, retrospective notes, and any names participants choose to enter.
- **Sprintraid:** the text you paste in to generate a RAID log, and the resulting entries.

**How it's used.** Solely to provide the features of the Software. We do **not** sell your data or use it for advertising.
